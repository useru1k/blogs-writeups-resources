### Learning

1. What is the concept that expresses the motivation for the action of the cyber attacker within the MITRE ATT&CK Framework? 
    a. Tactic
    b. Technique
    c. Procedure
    d. Mitigation
Answer: Tactic
In the MITRE ATT&CK framework, a Tactic represents the attacker’s motivation or goal—the “why” behind an action. Techniques are the “how,” procedures are specific implementations, and mitigations are defenses.
